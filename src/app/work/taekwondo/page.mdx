## Overview

The FHYT4 program is a unique wellness initiative that integrates Taekwondo, Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA), and generational experience to foster healthier physical and mental habits. By combining the discipline and physicality of Taekwondo, the evidence-based strategies of ABA, and the wisdom of intergenerational knowledge, FHYT4 empowers individuals to build resilience, improve fitness, and cultivate positive behavioral changes for lasting well-being.
